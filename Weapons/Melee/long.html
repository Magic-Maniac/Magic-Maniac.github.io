Long

  1. May stab and slash.
  2. Over 36" up to a maximum of 48".
  3. At least 2/3 of its length must be padded.
  4. If used to slash at least 2/3rds of its length must be Strike-Leagal.
  5. Allows for 1/3rd of its length to be subsituted for Heavy Padding.
  6. May be Double-Ended.
