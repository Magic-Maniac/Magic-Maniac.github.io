Seige Weapons

Siege Weapons are extremely powerful engines of destruction that, historically, were used for everything from anti-personnel, to tearing down walls from great distance. Examples of this type of weapon inculude ballistas, catapults, onagers, and trebuchets. The abilities and limitations of Siege Weapons are as follows:

  1. You may only have one siege weapon per twenty people on a team.
  2. Siege Weapons should have a historical or fantasy counterpart that they make mimic in form and function. Ammunition from Siege Weapons must conform to the following rules, and cannot have any additonal powers unles specified by the game designers:
    1. Players struckare subject to Engulfing Shield Destroying, Armor Destroying, Weapon Destroying, and Wounds Kill. Ignores Armor. Is stopped by the Enchantment Protection From Projectiles, but cannot be stopped by Missile Block.
    2. Siege Weapons that fire multiple projectiles simultaneously (such as a grapeshot catapult) count as hiring Armor Breaking projectiles. Each strike from a seperate projectile counts as an individual Armor Breaking strike. Both Protection From Projectiles and Missile Block may be used against these type of siege attacks. No effect on Shields.
    3. A Siege Weapon may not fire at a player within 20'.
    4. Siege Ammunition must, at minimum, conform to the standards of one of the following projectiles: Light Thrown, Heavy Thrown, Rocks, or Arrows.
  3. Siege Weapons must be approved by the game reeve prior to being used. Saftey is of the utmost concern when determining whether a Siege Weapon should be allowed on the field.
