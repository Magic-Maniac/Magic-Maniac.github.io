Javelins

Javelins ara a hybrid throwing/melee weapons. They are considered a melee wepon when wielded, but are considered a Projectile while in flight. Javelins are only avaliable to those classes explicitly granted Javelins.

  1. Between 36" and 72" long.
  2. Must have between 6" and 12" of Strike-Legal.
  3. Must be padded along their entire length.
  4. May be thrown and used in melee (including blocking and parrying).
  5. Stab only for both melee and throwing.
  6. Must strike point-first to score a hit.
  7. Must have a rigid core.
  8. Pommel is not required to meet the required to meet the requirements for a stabbing tip.
