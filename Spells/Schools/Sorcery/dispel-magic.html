Dispel Magic

Type: Verbal
Range: 20'

Incantation: "By My Power I Dispel That Magic"x3
Effect: All Enchantments on target are removed.
Note: Will work through Protection from Magic, Enlightened Soul, Sanctuary, and similar magic and abilities. Will work on players that are Frozen or Insubstantial.
