Reload

Type: Verbal
Range: Self

Incantation: "I Nocked My Arrows To My Bow, I Let Them Fly, My Quiver Is Low. Now I Pause To Go Reload"x3
Effect: Player becomes Out of Game and may move about the field retrieving their Arrows.
Limitations: Must stay at least 10' away from the other players at all times. The player may only remove their Out of Game state in the location they started by stating, "I Return With A Full Quiver"x3.
Note: May ask revee for assistance in retrieving Arrows that are within 10' of other players.
