Mass Healing

Type: Enchantment
Range: Self

Incantation: "Let The Powers Of Healing Flow Through Me"x3
Materials: Five Yellow Strips
Effect: Caster may Heal a player by touching them, stating "I Grant Thee Healing". Bearer must remove an Enchantment Strip after each use of Heal.
Note: Mass Healing is removed when the last strip is removed. The effect is not an incantation, and so is not stopped by Suppression, and may be used while moving, ect.
