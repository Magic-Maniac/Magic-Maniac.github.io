Battlefield Triage

Type: Enchantment
Range: Self or Touch: Others

Incantation: "Be A Bastion Of Healing"x3
Materials: Four Yellow Strips
Effect: Bearer is Stopped. Bearer may cast Greater Heal by announcing "<Player> Thou Art Made Whole". Bearer must remove an Enchantment Strip after each use of Greater Heal.
Note: Battlefield Triage is removed when the last strip is removed.
