Lightning Bolt

Type: Magic Ball
Range: Ball

Incantation: "The Flames Of Storms Is Mine To Evoke"x3
Materials: Yellow Magic Ball
Effect: A player struck is subject to Engulfing Stopped efect for 60 seconds. In addition, Lightning Bolt will have one of the following effects on the object first struck:

  1. A Weapon hit is destroyed.
  2. Armor hit with Armor Points remaining is subject to Armor Breaking.
  3. A player hit revieves a wound in that hit location.
