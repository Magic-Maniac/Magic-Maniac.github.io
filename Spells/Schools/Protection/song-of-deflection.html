Song of Deflection

Type: Enchantment
Range: Self

Incantation: "I Sing Of My Nimble Acrobatics"
Materials: No strip required
Effect: Bearer does not receive wounds from Ammounition, thrown Javelins, Rocks, and throwing weapons. Bearer must Chant "Song of Deflection" or sing a song regarding their acrobatic prowess. Singing in place of the normal Chant is still a Chant and must follow all Chant Rules.
