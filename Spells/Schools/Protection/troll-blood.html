Troll Blood

Type: Enchantment
Range: Touch: Others

Incantation: "The Blood Of The Trolls Sustains Thee"x3
Materials: Three White Strip
Effect: Enchanted player does not die as normal. When the player would otherwise die instead ignore triggering effect as though it had not occured, remove a strip, and become Frozen for 30 seconds. The bearer is treated as though they have the effects of Regeneration in addition to the above.
Note: Troll Blood is removed when the last strip is removed.
