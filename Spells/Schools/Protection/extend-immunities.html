Extend Immunities

Type: Enchantment
Range: Touch: Others

Incantation: Tie strip on target: "May The Blessing Of My God Protect Thee"x3
Materials: White Strip
Effect: The target player gains either Resistent to Command or Resistant to Death.
Limitaions: Type of ability must be chosen at the time of casting and may not be changed. The caster may only have one instance of Extend Immunities active at a time.
