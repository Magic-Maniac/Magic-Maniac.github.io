Adaptive Blessing

Type: Enchantment
Range: Touch: Others

Incantation: "I Enchant Thee With This Blessing"x3
Materials: White Strip
Effect: Bearer becomes Resistent to one of the following Schools: Death, Flame, Subdual, Command, Sorcery. School is chosen at the time of casting. Does not count towards a players Enchantment limit, may not be worn with any other Enchantments from the Protection School.
