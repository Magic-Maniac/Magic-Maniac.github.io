Discordia

Type: Enchantment
Range: Self

Incantation: "My Discordant Melodies Shall Stymie My Foes"x3
Materials: Red Strip
Effect: Tie on five Enchantment Strips. Bearer may cast Break Concentration by announcing "<Player> Thou Art Suppressed" and remove an Enchantment Strip. Enchantment is removed when the last strip is removed.
