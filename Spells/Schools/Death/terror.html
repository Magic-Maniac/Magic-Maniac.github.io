Terror

Type: Verbal
Range: 20'

Incantation: "Death Makes Thee Terrified"x3
Effect: Target may not attack or cast at the caster. Target must remain at least 50' away from the caster unless forced there by another magic or ability. Last 30 seconds.
Note: If the caster attacks or begins casting another magic at the target, this spell's effect is negated.
