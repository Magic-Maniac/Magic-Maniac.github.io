Corrosive Mist

Type: Enchantment
Range: Self or Touch: Others

Incantation: "The Mists Of Corrosion Surround Thee"x3
Materials: Five Red Strips
Effect: Bearer is Stopped. Bearer may cast Destroy Armor by announcing "<Player> The Mists Of Corrosion Destroy Your <amor location> Armor". Bearer must remove a strip after each use of Destry Armor.
Note: Corrosive Mist is removed when the last strip is removed.
