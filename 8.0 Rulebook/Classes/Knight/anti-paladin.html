Anti-Paladin

These are the standard bad guys of legend. Almost any adversary in a stock fantasy movie can qualify as an AntiPaladin, but historical cases abound – ranging from Vlad Dracul’s persecution of Turkish prisoners to Mongol warlords boiling opposing chieftains alive.

  • Examples: The Kurgan, Mordred, the Black Knight, the Nazgul, Sir Francis Dashwood
  • Garb: White belt with an openly displayed black phoenix on clothing or armor.
  • Requirements: Must be a knight
  • Look The Part: Awe 1/Life (m)
  • Armor: 4pts
  • Shields: Large
  • Weapons:All Melee, Javelins
